Yesterday was my turn to take over Danny's brilliant project: The Sheffield Parkour Diaries. "What's
it all about?", I hear you ask. Well, I'll tell you - Danny started this a while ago, and it's
basically a video journal of the day's training and general happenings throughout the day's jam, and
what we do is take it in turn, each person does a block of four diaries and then it gets passed on
to someone else (don't ask how that came to week 11...)

Yesterday's jam was very eventful, and regardless of the fact that there's hardly any parkour in the
video, we got a fair bit of training done, including the huge achievement of mine and Will's of the
standing arm jump at the end. I used Danny's "Parkour Rap" in the vid, see his myspace for more
songs.

The video includes a dedication to Shane's brother Danny, who, as many of you will know, died two
weeks ago at the age of 16.

## Next Week: Cliffhanger

Cliffhanger is billed as "the outdoor event for outdoor people" and takes place next weekend (14th &
15th July) in Graves Park - there'll be come-and-try-it climbing walls, high ropes courses, the
British Bouldering Championships, a World Record Dyno Competition and many other outdoor activities
and such. The Sheffield Parkour guys will be running a demo on a purpose-built course and showing
the public what parkour's all about. I'll be filming for next week's diary: Week 12. See here next
week for the video.
